    This revealed a lot of blind spots that were previously unknown to us. This is where our wisdom springs from. We know the drill and we will be happy to apply our wisdom to your organization.

We enjoy solving business problems and configuring creative solutions to relieve you from your pain points and meet your objectives. Every day is a new day to learn something new in Dayforce. The amazing ecosystem of Support, KB articles, Release updates, Customer Success webinars, Compliance Updates, Regulatory Changes, New Announcements, Product Roadmaps, Product Guides and the plethora of documentation are voraciously consumed by us, so we can remain reliable and relevant for all times.

That is wisdom sharing and that is what Kandha is all about. This we do, because we deeply care for our clients and their employees - Making your work lives better!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take for Kandha to actively engage with a client post contract signing stage?

    It depends on the number of projects our consultants are working on at that time. Rest assured; we will get our consultants to actively engage as soon as we can.

  • Does Kandha offer customized client specific training or generic training?

    We review our clients’ set-up and create unique training plans based on their needs. We also create internal change management processes with your existing tools, or even with just an existing shared email set-up to create an incident management protocol that can keep you abreast of day-to-day inquiries from the operations and employees.

  • Can we contact Kandha on a need only basis like clients reaching out to Ceridian support?

    Yes, our support emails are regularly monitored. We will respond to emails within 24-48 hours. Our sales process is extremely simplified, you do not have to order a "minimum" number of hours to engage with us. We only charge based on hours spent resolving an issue.

  • Can Kandha act as a Project Manager for our implementation?

    Yes, we offer project management services for net new implementations and Mergers & Acquisitions. Our unique D4S methodology guarantees near perfect go-lives. We say "near perfect" because we under promise and over deliver.

  • Can Kandha help with Benefit open enrollments each year?

    Yes, our consultants have experience with managing open enrollments for any sized organizations. We can help configure and deploy both web and mobile based open enrollment experiences for your employees.
